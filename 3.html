Ordered List of Steps for Tea

  1. Boil water in a kettle.
  2. Place a tea bag in a mug.
  3. Pour hot water over the tea bag.
  4. Steep for 3–5 minutes.
  5. Add sugar or milk to taste.
  6. Remove the tea bag and enjoy.
